NIRVANA FanPage

Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. It was founded by lead singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic.
Nirvana went through a succession of drummers, the longest-lasting and best-known being Dave Grohl, who joined in 1990. Though the band dissolved in 1994 after the
death of Cobain, their music maintains a popular following and continues to influence modern rock and roll culture.

Band Members

  • Kurt Cobain
  • David Grohl
  • Krist Novoselic
